Chocolate Fudge Recipe

This is the original chocolate fudge recipe before any CSS styling was applied.

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Combine sugar, milk, and butter in a saucepan over medium heat.

2. Stir continuously until the mixture comes to a boil.

3. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips and vanilla.

4. Pour into a greased pan and let cool before cutting into squares.
